Welcome to Task Manager!

This application helps you manage your tasks and important dates efficiently. You can create a to-do list, mark important dates on the calendar, and customize your experience through the settings.

Features:

  • To-Do List: Keep track of tasks you need to complete.
  • Calendar: Mark important dates and reminders.
  • Settings: Personalize your app experience.
  • Help: Get assistance on how to use the app.

Get started by navigating to the To-Do List or Calendar sections!

      Help Section

      If you need assistance using this Task Manager, here's how to get started:

      • To-Do List: Enter a task in the input box and click "Add Task". You can check off completed tasks.
      • Calendar: Use the date picker to select an important date and click "Mark Important Date" to save it.
      • Settings: Customize the app's appearance. Click "Save Settings" to apply changes.
      • Feedback: Your feedback is important! Please share any suggestions or issues.
